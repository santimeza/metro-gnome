JavaScript Synthesizer Examples

  • Simple Synth with Pitch Shift
  • Keyboard Whole Tone Chords
  • Keyboard Diatonic Chords
Follow the instructions in each example to generate audio. To see the code, use "Show Page Source" or similar in your browser. Alternatively, download the HTML files and run them locally.

- Simple Synth with Pitch Shift: A symple synthesizer that plays a constant tone on activation. Shift the pitch of the tone with the slider or keys, mute options avaliable.

- Whole Tone Chords: Plays triad of the whole tone scale using the keyboard.

- Diatonic Chords: Plays the diatonic chords of a key. Change the key with the drop menu, as well as wave type, and chord type.
