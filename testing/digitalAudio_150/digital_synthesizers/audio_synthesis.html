Simple JavaScript Synthesizers

Follow the instructions in each example to generate audio. To see the code, use "Show Page Source" or similar in your browser. Alternatively, download the HTML files and run them locally.

Simple Synth with Pitch Shift Keyboard Whole Tone Chords Keyboard Diatonic Chords

Simple Synth with Pitch Shift: A simple synthesizer that plays a constant tone on activation. Shift the pitch of the tone with the slider or keys, mute options available.

Whole Tone Chords: Plays triads of the whole tone scale using the keyboard.

Diatonic Chords: Plays the diatonic chords of a key. Change the key with the drop-down menu, as well as wave type, and chord type.
