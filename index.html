Metro-gnome

Select a click sound, adjust BPM, and click Start to begin the metronome. For timing feedback, click the space bar on every beat, or tap the box for mobile. The white rectangle will flash red if you are rushing or dragging and green if your timing is correct. Click the checkboxes on the right to allow the feedback box to take into account eighth notes and eighth note triplets. (Metro-Gnome counts quarter notes)
